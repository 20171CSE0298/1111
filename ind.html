lanyards

A lanyard is a cord or strap worn around the neck to carry such items as keys or identification cards. Lanyards are commonly used to display badges, tickets or ID cards for identification where security is required, such as Educational institutions, businesses, Hospitals, and used in the entertainment industry. Such lanyards are often made of braided or woven fabric or split with a clip attached to the end.
